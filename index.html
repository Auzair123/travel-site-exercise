Welcome to Portugal

Portugal welcomes visitors with its status as Europe's oldest nation with stable borders, a reputation as one of the world's safest and most peaceful countries, and incredibly friendly locals.

Highlights

Oldest Nation: Portugal has had largely the same borders since the 13th century, making it Europe's oldest nation-state.
Lisbon Older Than Rome: The capital, Lisbon, predates Rome and is one of the oldest cities in Western Europe.
